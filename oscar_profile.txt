EMERGENCY INFORMATION

Primary vet: Dr. Sarah Chen — City Pet Clinic — available Monday to Saturday 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
Emergency vet: Dr. James Wilson — handles emergency and after-hours consultations.

Symptoms that require an immediate emergency vet visit:
- Straining to urinate or no urine output for more than 12 hours (urinary blockage — life threatening in male cats)
- Difficulty breathing or breathing with mouth open
- Sudden paralysis of the back legs
- Collapse or unresponsiveness
- Suspected poisoning, especially from plants (lilies are extremely toxic to cats — even small amounts cause kidney failure)
- Seizure lasting more than 2 minutes
