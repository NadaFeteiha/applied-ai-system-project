EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE
Dr. Michael Patel earned his DVM from Tufts University Cummings School of Veterinary Medicine and completed a 3-year internal medicine residency at the Animal Medical Center in New York City. He is a Diplomate of the American College of Veterinary Internal Medicine (DACVIM). He has 14 years of experience, with a particular focus on aging pets and complex multi-system disease. He is known for comprehensive workups and for finding answers when generalist vets are stumped.

ABOUT DR. PATEL
Dr. Patel has a quiet, reassuring manner that puts both pets and their owners at ease. He is particularly passionate about senior pet wellness — helping older dogs and cats maintain quality of life for as long as possible. He runs a highly regarded Senior Pet Wellness Program that includes customized health panels for pets over 7 years old. His clinic has an in-house laboratory for rapid blood and urine analysis, digital radiology, and abdominal ultrasound.

SENIOR PET WELLNESS PROGRAM (DOGS & CATS 7+)
The Downtown Pet Wellness Senior Program includes:
- Comprehensive physical exam
- Complete blood count (CBC)
- Full chemistry panel (liver, kidney, blood sugar, electrolytes)
- Thyroid level check
- Urinalysis
- Blood pressure measurement
- Nutritional assessment
- Joint mobility evaluation
All included in the $180 Senior Wellness Panel visit. Highly recommended every 6 months for pets over 10 years old.

COMMON CONDITIONS MANAGED

- Diabetes mellitus (Type 1 and insulin-dependent)
- Hyperthyroidism and hypothyroidism
- Inflammatory bowel disease (IBD)
- Liver disease and hepatic lipidosis
- Respiratory conditions (asthma, bronchitis, collapsing trachea)
- Cushing's disease (hyperadrenocorticism)
- Pancreatitis
- Cardiac arrhythmias and early heart disease

NOTES
Dr. Patel does not see exotic species. His clinic is best suited for dogs and cats, particularly those with ongoing complex health conditions or those entering their senior years.
