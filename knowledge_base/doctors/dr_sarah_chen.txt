EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE
Dr. Sarah Chen graduated from UC Davis School of Veterinary Medicine with honors. She has 12 years of clinical experience treating dogs, cats, and exotic animals. She completed a specialized externship in avian and exotic animal medicine at the San Diego Zoo. She is a member of the Association of Exotic Mammal Veterinarians (AEMV) and the American Veterinary Medical Association (AVMA).

ABOUT DR. CHEN
Dr. Chen is known for her calm bedside manner and thorough approach to diagnostics. She is fluent in Mandarin and English and welcomes clients from diverse backgrounds. She has a special passion for treating exotic animals that many general vets turn away. Her clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art imaging including digital X-ray and ultrasound.
