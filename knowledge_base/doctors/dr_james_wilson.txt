EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE
Dr. James Wilson earned his DVM from Cornell University College of Veterinary Medicine and completed a 3-year surgical residency at the University of Pennsylvania. He is a Diplomate of the American College of Veterinary Surgeons (DACVS), a board certification held by fewer than 1% of veterinarians. He has 18 years of surgical experience and has performed over 5,000 procedures. He is a published researcher in veterinary orthopedic surgery.

ABOUT DR. WILSON
Dr. Wilson is widely regarded as one of the top veterinary surgeons in the region. His calm precision and meticulous technique have saved many animals that were considered inoperable. He works closely with referring veterinarians to ensure continuity of care. His clinic has a state-of-the-art surgical suite, dedicated recovery ward, and 24-hour post-surgical monitoring.

HOW TO BOOK AN APPOINTMENT WITH DR. WILSON
1. A referral from your primary care veterinarian is strongly recommended (but not always required)
2. Call the surgery center: (555) 345-6789, Monday through Saturday
3. Email referrals to referrals@sunrisevetsurgery.com
4. Emergency surgical consults can be arranged by calling the main line
5. First-time patients need to provide previous medical records and X-rays prior to the consultation appointment
6. Online booking is available for consultation appointments at www.sunrisevetsurgery.com/appointments

INSURANCE & PAYMENT
Dr. Wilson's center accepts all major pet insurance carriers. Due to the cost of surgical procedures, payment plans are available through CareCredit and Scratchpay. A deposit (typically 50%) is required to schedule surgical procedures. Full payment is due before the patient is discharged.

PATIENT TYPES ACCEPTED
Dogs and cats (primary). Will see rabbits and ferrets for surgical cases on a case-by-case basis.

PREPARATION FOR SURGERY
- Most surgeries require fasting: no food 12 hours before surgery, water until 2 hours before
- Bring all previous X-rays, lab results, and veterinary records
- Arrange for a responsible adult to transport the pet home after surgery
- Post-operative care instructions will be provided in detail before discharge

NOTES
Dr. Wilson does not perform routine wellness exams. He is a specialist exclusively focused on surgical cases. Please contact your primary care veterinarian for routine care and referrals.
