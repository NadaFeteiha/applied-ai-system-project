EDUCATION & EXPERIENCE
Dr. Emily Rodriguez received her DVM from the University of Florida College of Veterinary Medicine and completed a specialized 3-year dermatology residency at North Carolina State University. She is a Diplomate of the American College of Veterinary Dermatology (DACVD). She has 9 years of experience managing complex skin and allergy cases in companion animals. She has lectured at national veterinary conferences on atopic dermatitis management.

ABOUT DR. RODRIGUEZ
Dr. Rodriguez is passionate about improving quality of life for pets suffering from chronic skin conditions and allergies. She takes a comprehensive, evidence-based approach to diagnostics including intradermal allergy testing, skin biopsies, and cultures. She is particularly skilled at developing customized immunotherapy (allergy shots or drops) plans for dogs with seasonal and environmental allergies. She is known for her clear communication and willingness to educate pet owners about long-term management.

NOTES FOR PATIENTS
Allergy testing is a multi-step process that often spans several visits. Dr. Rodriguez will set realistic expectations and provide a detailed management plan at the first visit. Results from allergy testing take 2 to 3 weeks to process. Evening appointments on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday are especially popular — book these early.
